## 📥 Installation Guide

Eternity Chrono offers two seamless ways to install on your Android device:

### Method 1: The Smart Web Install (Recommended)
Get the app directly from your browser without downloading any files:
1. Open the **[Live App Link](https://eternity-009.github.io/Eternity-Chrono/)** in Google Chrome on your Android phone.
2. Tap the **3-dots menu (⋮)** in the top right corner of Chrome.
3. Select **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Enjoy the standalone native experience!

### Method 2: Direct APK Download
For users who prefer managing installation files:
1. Go to our **[Latest Releases](https://github.com/Eternity-009/Eternity-Chrono/releases/latest)** page.
2. Scroll down to the **Assets** section.
3. Download the `Eternity-Chrono.apk` file.
4. Install it on your device (Grant "Install from unknown sources" if prompted).
